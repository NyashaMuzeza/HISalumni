Meet Dave

Chatbot

It's a Whatsapp chatbot-based solution designed to address the college counseling struggles faced by African students

About Dave

In a region where access to college counseling services is often limited, Dave offers an easy-to-use,scalable, and entirely free platform.

It empowers low-income students to access counseling and prepare effectively for college applications

The Core Idea

Dave is a comprehensive system crafted to guide students, regardless of their starting point, from novices in college research to experts in networking, essay writing, personal branding, and other skills essential for the college application journey.

Who is it for?

Dave caters primarily to rural undergraduate scholarship applicants individuals seeking to enhance their writing skills making it accessible via WhatsApp

The aim

The HIS Alumni aims to make a significant impact on the annual college application process for a substantial portion of the student population with Dave.

Key Features

Resource Dissemination

Dave boasts access to an extensive database of resources, offering students access to documents such as admissions officers' notes from Ivy League universities, personal anecdotes from students at MIT, Harvard, Princeton, and more. It also provides guides for applying for financial aid, including applications specific to supported schools.
